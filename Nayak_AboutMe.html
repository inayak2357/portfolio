Isha Nayak

Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Isha Nayak, a freshman at Dublin High School. I am interested in going into the engineering field as a 3D animator.

I am currently taking the elective course Computer Science Principles and I hope to broaden my knowledge and interests in the engineering field by taking courses outside of this course. A few of the websites I use for self learning include w3schools.com and Codecademy.

One of the careers I am drawn to is 3D animation since I have an interest in drawing. I love to draw and the many aspects of the creative process involved in drawing. For example, because of this interest I have taken Khan Academy courses about what shading artists at Pixar do through the partnership between Khan Academy and Pixar in a Box. I look forward to learning and furthering my interest in 3D animation, and also look forward to working with others.

Over the years I have learned that friends and teammates provide strong support and through good communication and loyalty projects/assignments can get done more efficiently. In middle school I was a part of a club called the California Junior Scholarship Federation where I project managed the Animals and Environment project group. I played a big part in organizing a popsicle fundraiser to raise money for the Hurricane Harvey victims where we raised over $500 for the victims of the Hurricane Harvey. Through the fundraiser I learned about working with others and communicating the dates through flyers and emails. I also learned about planning to keep the fundraiser in order.

In addition to self-learning and being a team player, I believe being creative is crucial to success. While I learn a lot through my classes and teammates at school, I continue to apply my skills whenever possible. In the past I have created flyers for fundraisers for the Animals and Environment Project Group for CJSF (California Junior Scholarship Federation).

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me, please feel free to explore the rest of my website.
